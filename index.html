About TeensPray

Teens Pray is a Movement that started in 2019 and has a specific mandate to Teach and Groom Teenagers in the Doctrine and Art of Prayer for a fulfilling Christian adventure in the end time.

Teens Pray is an interdenominational meeting that has captured hundreds of Teenagers from various denominations in Nigeria and it gets bigger by the year. Caleb Oladiti the Convener of Teens Pray alongside his team is set to fulfill God's desire for the rise a Praying Generation. See you at Teens Pray 2023.
